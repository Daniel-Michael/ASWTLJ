Why I love motorcycles

Date: 06/04/2019

- "The rush never fades"

by Daniel Michael

Warning: nothing but motorcycles ahead:

I think it's fair to say I love riding motorcycles, it's that simple. I have done since I was about four years old when my Dad bought me a PeeWee 80, a small CC automatic clutch motorcycle to learn on.

To me, riding motorcycles has become something of a way of life, I prefer to ride over driving a car in any situation, and I like short rides to the shops as well as long haul rides across country, or overseas.

I also like web design and development and have run a couple of different motorcycle focussed websites over the years, starting with Ducati Blog, then Daily Bikers and most recently a more community focussed site where you can upload your own images and start a profile, talk with others etc. called Moto Honcho.

I guess you could call it something of an obsession.

Some of the bikes I've had over the years include:

  1. KTM 300
  2. Honda Grom MSX 125
  3. Suzuki DRZ 400e
  4. Buell XB12X
  5. BMW S1000R
  6. Ducati Scrambler Desert Sled
  7. BMW R1200GS

KTM's are always orange

Ducatis are always red

BMWs remind me of the colour blue.

I own a BMW and a Ducati.

It doesn't really matter what bike you ride, as long as you ride a bike, we can be mates.
